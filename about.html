Why Choose Manchitra

The Four Pillars of Our Practice

We never compromise on equipment quality, data integrity, or client deadlines.

1. DGPS / RTK Satellite Superiority

We utilize multi-frequency Differential GPS instrumentation that communicates directly with global satellite constellations, eliminating cumulative human chain-measurement errors.

2. Revenue Map Proficiency

Our team possesses deep expertise in decoding historical Shajra, Musavi, and Aks-Shajra revenue maps, overlaying them onto real-world CAD coordinates.

3. Rapid Turnaround Times

Time is money in real estate development. We process field datasets immediately upon survey completion, delivering digital `.DWG` and `.PDF` plans within 24 to 48 hours.

4. Unwavering Ethics & Transparency

As noted in verified client testimonials, we operate with transparent quotations and unbiased field measurements that stand up to rigorous scrutiny.
