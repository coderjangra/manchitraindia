Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered by our seasoned surveying specialists in Panchkula.

1. What is the difference between DGPS and traditional Total Station surveying?
Differential GPS (DGPS/RTK) uses satellite constellations combined with ground corrections to determine global coordinates with centimeter accuracy over large distances instantly. Total Stations use laser distance meters and angles, ideal for high-precision building layouts and dense tree coverage where GPS signals might be obstructed. Manchitra deploys both simultaneously.
2. How long does a standard boundary demarcation survey take?
For standard residential plots (up to 1 acre) in Panchkula, Chandigarh, or Mohali, field surveying and pillar fixing typically takes 1 day. Complete AutoCAD drawings and certified survey reports are delivered within 24 to 48 hours.
3. Do you assist in legal boundary disputes or revenue map verification?
Yes. We regularly work alongside property owners, legal counsel, and revenue authorities to reconcile ground realities with official government Shajra/Musavi maps, identifying exact encroachment overlaps.
